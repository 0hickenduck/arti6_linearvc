VTuber Voice Pipeline

Automated dataset curation for voice conversion research. From raw YouTube streams to clean, segmented ML training data in minutes.

View Live Demo View on GitHub

How It Works

1

Discovery & Download

Uses Gemini Flash to analyze channels and isolate Speech vs Singing content, downloading tracks automatically with yt-dlp.

2

Separation

Applies Demucs to strip background music, sound effects, and game noise, leaving only raw vocals.

3

VAD & Segmentation

Utilizes Silero VAD to slice the continuous vocal track into clean 3-15 second phonetic chunks ideal for training.
