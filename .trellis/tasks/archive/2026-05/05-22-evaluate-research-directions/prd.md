## Technical Approach
**Focus:** Controlling Cross-Domain Timbre Shift (Speaking vs. Singing, L1 vs. L2).
**Pipeline:**
1. Use an off-the-shelf speaker encoder (ECAPA-TDNN/WavLM) to extract embeddings.
2. **Method A:** Compute linear steering vectors (Difference of Means) between domains.
3. **Method B:** Train a lightweight neural network (MLP) to predict the domain-shifted embedding from a base embedding.
4. Pass the modified embedding to a frozen TTS/VC decoder to synthesize the result.

  - **Novelty:** Combining Latent Steering (A) to explicitly model/solve the Cross-lingual/Singing Timbre Shift (B) is a very strong, highly novel angle for a Master's thesis.
